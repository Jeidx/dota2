Earthshaker

Raigor Stonehoof, the Earthshaker, is a melee strength hero with several area of effect (AoE) disables, commonly played as a ganker or initiator.

Crystal Maiden

Rylai, the Crystal Maiden, is a ranged intelligence hero who uses the power of frost and ice to disable and dispatch her foes.

Ogre Magi

Aggron Stonebreak, the Ogre Magi, is a melee intelligence hero, famous for his random potential for extreme burst damage and his enormous health pool for an intelligence hero.

Shadow Shaman

Rhasta, the Shadow Shaman, is a ranged intelligence hero mostly played as a pusher and disabler, wielding abilities that make pushing lanes more efficient.
